Are there Multiple Killers?

  1. Since 1997, the bodies of at least 10 men have been pulled from rivers in and around the city of La Crosse.

  2. Chicago, Minneapolis, and Milwaukee also have cases of young men turning up dead in the water.

  3. In New Jersey, 3 bodies of young men were pulled from the Hudson river between 2014 and 2016.

  4. In England, 61 bodies of mostly young men, were found in the canals of Greater Manchester between 2008 and 2014.
