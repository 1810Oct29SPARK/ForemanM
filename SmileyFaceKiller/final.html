Smiley Face Killers Debunked

The police and also several scientists came up with 18 facts of why the smiley face killer does not exist, below are just some of the highlights.

  • The victims bodies show no evidence of trauma or drugging
  • There is no motives or hard evidence linking the deaths together
  • The smiley faces found near the bodies are not consistent with one another, haven't been proven to be made around the times of the deaths, and don't occur within close proximity to the death scenes.
